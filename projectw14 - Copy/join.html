Reasons to Join the Chamber of Commerce







Chamber of commerce membership benefits

• By joining a chamber of commerce, you can get discounts on many of your business needs, insurances, office supplies, shipping, services, marketing, accounting software, and payroll services, and more. • Being a member of the Chamber of Commerce increases your credibility and makes your business more attractive to customers. • At the Chamber of commerce, we advocate for businesses and represent them before the government at the municipal, provincial and federal levels on issues that affect business in our community. When you join the Chamber, you know that someone has your back and you can also speak up to address the issues that concern you. • If we receive inquiries from the group or from outside the Chamber, we always refer members first, using our online membership list. That means more leads for you more money in your bank account. • When you join the Chamber you’ll receive a welcome package which includes messages on our social channels, and in our bi-weekly eNews. Plus you’ll be automatically listed on our online member directory which has a number of updatable features, so other members and community residents can easily search for and locate your business information. this represents many promotional opportunities. • As a member, you will have more chances to network with other members of the camara, you will have more opportunities to meet fellow community members and mingle with potential partners and customers.
