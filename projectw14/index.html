Come and visit us


Idaho is a mountainous state in the northwest United States with large swaths of preserved wilderness and outdoor recreation areas. Boise, Idaho's capital, is nestled in the foothills of the Rocky Mountains and is separated by the Boise River, which is famous for rafting and fishing. Julia Davis Park, on the city's riverfront, is a downtown green space with a rose garden, museums, and a zoo.

Come and have fun, sledding, climbing snowy mountains, excursions, ice skating and of course enjoying the resort or the small towns around with its variety of B & B.
