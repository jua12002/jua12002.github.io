Winnipeg is the capital of Manitoba in Canada, it is usually called winterpeg for the extreme cold in winter, reaching temperatures down to -55°C. The city is surrounded by the Red and Assiniboine rivers. This is a multicultural city and has many malls, shops and restaurants, with a lot of many green areas and parks, but the most exiting is that has more than 100,000 lakes.
