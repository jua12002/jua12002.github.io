Have tons of fun with all the water activities

Fish Haven, originally named Rush Creek, is an unincorporated community along the shores of Bear Lake in Bear Lake County, Idaho, United States. It is 4 km north of the Utah border. Wikipedia

There are so many things to do at the lake, in any season of the year. So, let get out and enjoy the cool water, there are so many fun things to do at the lake, as swimming keeps you cool, boating with friend or family, Jet skiing is a wonderful activity at the lake, kayaks and canoes are great activities to enjoy the nature, fishing is another amazing activity at the lake.

If the water is too cold, you can sunbathing, or tubing can be another activity with a lot of fun, or even have a picnic in front of the lake.
